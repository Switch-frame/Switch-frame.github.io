About US

Helping Each Other can Make World Better

Yuvana Trust is a dedicated non-governmental organization (NGO) based in Konaje, Mangalore, India. Our primary focus is on a wide range of charitable activities aimed at making a significant impact in our community. We are committed to environmental conservation, healthcare support, and educational initiatives. One of our major efforts is supporting underprivileged school children. We offer educational resources, scholarships, and mentoring programs to ensure these children have access to quality education and opportunities for a brighter future. In addition, we provide comprehensive assistance to individuals with disabilities. This includes offering medical support, resources for daily living, and programs to enhance their quality of life and integration into society. Moreover, we are passionate about organizing beach clean-up drives. These drives not only help in keeping our local beaches pristine but also raise awareness about the importance of environmental conservation among the community. Our aim is to create sustainable and positive change in the community through these focused initiatives and activities.

Mission Statement

Yuvana Trust(R) is dedicated to transforming lives and communities through sustainable development, education, and healthcare initiatives. We strive to create a compassionate and equitable world by empowering individuals, protecting the environment, and providing essential support to those in need. Our mission is to inspire positive change and build a brighter future for all.
